    Step 1: Understanding YoiGuide

    Get to know the core features and how to make the most of YoiGuide.

    a. Core Features

    YoiGuide allows you to track and manage your Genshin Impact resources. This includes setting goals, tracking progress, and optimizing your resources.

    b. Setting Goals

    Set up specific milestones and achievements you wish to reach in Genshin Impact. This could be collecting certain resources, leveling up characters, or achieving specific tasks.

    c. Tracking Progress

    Monitor your progress in real-time. See what resources you have, what you need, and plan accordingly.
